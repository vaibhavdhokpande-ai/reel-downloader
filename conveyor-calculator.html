📐 Formula Reference
Friction Force
F_f = f × L × (m_b + m_l) × g × cos(θ)
Gravity Component
F_g = (m_b + m_l) × g × sin(θ) × L
Effective Tension
T_e = F_f + F_g
Drive Power
P = (T_e × v) / η
Belt Capacity
Q = m_l × v × 3.6 (t/h)
Tight-Side Tension
T₁ = T₂ + T_e
