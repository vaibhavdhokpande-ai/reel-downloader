Quick Guide
How To Download
1

Install the APK

Download and install TaskJunction on your Android device. Enable "Unknown Sources" in settings if prompted.

2

Copy the Reel Link

Open Instagram, Facebook, or YouTube. Tap Share on any reel and copy the link.

3

Paste & Download

Open TaskJunction, paste the link in the search bar, and tap Download. Done in seconds!

4

Enjoy Offline

Your reel is saved in HD to your gallery. Watch, share, or repost anytime — no internet needed.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. Information We Collect

TaskJunction does not collect any personal information. We do not require you to create an account or log in.

2. How the App Works

Our app processes publicly available reel links provided by the user. All downloads happen locally on your device. We do not store, transmit, or share any video content or user data on our servers.

3. Permissions Used

  • Internet Access: Required to fetch video data from the link you provide.
  • Storage Access: Required to save downloaded videos to your device gallery.
